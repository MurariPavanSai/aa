Prediction

Reach analysis typically involves examining metrics such as impressions, views, or unique visitors to determine the extent of reach. Impressions refer to the total number of times a post or content was displayed, while views indicate the number of times a specific piece of content was seen by users. By analyzing these metrics, businesses can gauge the overall reach of their Instagram posts and understand how many people their content has potentially reached.
